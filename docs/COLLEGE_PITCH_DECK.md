## Slide 3 — The Problem We Identified

Through industry engagements and hiring experiences, we observed a significant disconnect between skills students acquire through traditional education and the practical skills demanded by employers. Many graduates possess theoretical knowledge but lack hands-on experience, industry exposure, and professional readiness.

| Challenge | Impact |

| Theory-heavy curriculum | Students struggle in TCS/Infosys/product company interviews |

| Placement gap | 2nd–4th year students lack Git, Linux, APIs, real projects |

**Bottom line:** Colleges need a **scalable, measurable, industry-aligned** internship layer — not another PDF syllabus.

## Slide 5 — Student Journey (Visual)

```
Enroll → Pick Track → Weekly Modules → Lessons + Labs → 50-Q Quiz (80% pass)
    → Mini Projects → Interview Prep → Capstone → Certificate → Placement-ready portfolio
```

**Weekly rhythm (per student):**
1. **12–15 deep-dive topics** with CLI commands, diagrams, industry scenarios  
2. **Hands-on labs** in Monaco editor (run code in browser)  
3. **Weekly live session** — mentor Q&A with students; recorded sessions shared on platform  
4. **AI assistant** for lesson-specific doubts (disabled during quizzes)  
5. **Weekly assessment** — 50 questions (MCQ, debugging, output prediction, scenarios)  
6. **Interview module** — industry Q&A, mock dialogue, unpredictable-question playbook
